Gracious Giving Foundation

Founded in 2007 by Pastor (Mrs.) Funke Adebayo, Gracious Giving Foundation is a faith-based charitable organization dedicated to supporting widows, vulnerable families, and the less privileged, particularly in Nigeria. The Foundation was established to demonstrate God's love through practical acts of compassion, generosity, and community service.

Giving to widows and the needy is at the heart of the Foundation's mission. Through financial assistance, food distribution, encouragement, and community outreach programs, Gracious Giving Foundation continues to bring hope, dignity, and relief to those facing hardship.

"Honor widows who are truly in need." — 1 Timothy 5:3

During one of her visits to Nigeria, Pastor Funke Adebayo spent time with widows supported by the Foundation, blessing them with cash gifts, food supplies, and words of encouragement. It was a joyful and memorable occasion that reflected the Foundation's commitment to honoring and caring for widows.

As a Minister of the Gospel, philanthropist, and transformational community leader, Pastor Funke Adebayo leads with grace, compassion, and purpose. Her vision is to empower lives, restore hope, and build stronger communities through faith-driven service and humanitarian outreach.

Today, Gracious Giving Foundation continues to impact lives, offering practical support to those in need and inspiring others to make a difference through acts of kindness and generosity.
